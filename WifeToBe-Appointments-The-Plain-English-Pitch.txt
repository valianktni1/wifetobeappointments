THE ONE-LINER
------------------------------------------------------------------------
It's your own private booking website AND your behind-the-scenes diary,
rolled into one — beautiful for your brides, effortless for your staff,
and it costs you nothing per booking because you own it.


THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Right now, bookings mean phone calls, texts, a paper diary, double-booked
fitting rooms, and no-shows. Brides ring during your busiest fitting and
you're juggling a pen while pinning a dress. This fixes all of that.

Your customers book themselves, day or night. Your diary fills in
automatically. Nobody gets double-booked. And you get a tidy, searchable
record of every appointment across BOTH boutiques.


WHAT YOUR CUSTOMERS SEE  (the pretty bit)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A calm, elegant booking page that looks exactly like your website — same
fonts, same colours, same feel. In a few taps a bride can:

  * Pick the boutique she wants (Warrington or Runcorn).
  * Choose what she's coming in for (a fitting, a consultation, etc.).
  * See a lovely calendar with your closed days greyed out.
  * Pick from ONLY the times that are genuinely free.
  * Leave her details and answer any questions you want to ask
    (wedding date, dress size, budget, "how did you hear about us?").
  * Get an instant confirmation with a reference number, and add it
    straight to her own phone calendar with one tap.

If she changes her mind, she can reschedule or cancel herself using her
reference — no awkward phone call, no chasing you.

And if her preferred day is fully booked? Instead of losing her, she can
join a WAITLIST so you can call her the moment a space opens up.


WHAT YOU & YOUR STAFF GET  (the powerful bit)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A private, password-protected control panel that runs the whole show:

  YOUR DAY AT A GLANCE
    Log in and instantly see today's appointments and a running count of
    what's coming up, what's pending, and how busy each boutique is.

  THE DIARY, SORTED
    Every booking in one place. Search and filter by shop or status.
    Open any one to see full details and the customer's answers. Confirm
    it, mark it done, cancel it, add a private note, or move it to a new
    day and time — all in a couple of clicks.

  YOU'RE ALWAYS IN CONTROL OF YOUR TIME
    Set your opening hours for each day of the week, per boutique. Say how
    many appointments can run at once (e.g. two fitting rooms = two at a
    time) and add a gap between them for cleardown. Block out whole days
    for holidays, or just an hour for lunch. The system will never offer
    a bride a time you can't actually do.

  MAKE IT YOURS
    Add your own photos, opening-hours text and a friendly "what to
    expect" note for each shop. Decide exactly what services you offer and
    how long each takes. Build your own booking questions. No developer
    needed — you change it yourself, whenever you like.

  EXPORT & SHARE
    Pull all your bookings into a spreadsheet in one click. Or plug the
    diary straight into your Google or Apple Calendar so every appointment
    turns up on your phone automatically.


NO MORE NO-SHOWS & MISSED MESSAGES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The system can automatically email your brides — a confirmation when they
book, another when you confirm it, and a friendly reminder the day before.
It emails you (or the shop) too, so a new booking never slips through.
It all sends from YOUR own email address, and every message is switched
OFF until you decide to turn it on — so there are never any surprises.


BUILT FOR A TEAM
------------------------------------------------------------------------
You (the owner) are the boss account — the only one who can add or remove
staff and change the big settings. You can add up to four staff logins,
each with their own password, their own email, and optional extra security
(the same Google Authenticator app banks use). Your owner account can
never be locked out or deleted by accident.


WHY IT BEATS THE "£X-A-MONTH" BOOKING APPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * NO monthly subscription. NO fee per booking. You own it outright.
  * Your data lives on YOUR hardware — not on someone else's cloud.
  * It looks like YOUR brand, not a generic booking widget.
  * It already runs TWO shops out of the box, and can be re-skinned for
    any other boutique or business with very little work.
  * Nothing to install for your staff — it's just a website they log into.


ROCK-SOLID & SAFE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Passwords are scrambled and stored safely — never in plain text.
  * Optional two-step login for extra peace of mind.
  * It quietly prevents two brides grabbing the same slot at the same
    second.
  * Runs on your own server, backed up automatically every night, with a
    month of backups kept just in case.

It turns booking from a phone-and-paper headache into a polished, always-
